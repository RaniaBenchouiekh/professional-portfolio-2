About me

I'm Zina and I'm

Hello ! I’m Zina, I’m 20 years old, and I’m a second year English student at the University of Algiers 2 I’m a communication department member of the university’s very first official club, WATI. My first choice of my field of study was architecture, which I studied for a year, before switching to an English major.

I’m also a polyglot, language learning is my biggest passion. Other than the languages I already speak, which are Arabic (both MSA and the Algerian dialect), French, English and Spanish, I’m learning German, Italian, Kabyle and Korean. I’m also a podcaster. I host my own language learning podcast, entitled Dz Polyglot.

When I’m not learning languages, I’m most probably reading. I’m very passionate about literature and history. This led me to build my own community of thousands of readers on Instagram, under the username Dz Book Dealer. It’s an account for book exchanges on all the Algerian territory.

I currently work as a freelance writer, and an online English teacher. I also have some experience working as a translator and a transcriber.

Other interests of mine include fields such as sociology and psychology. I also enjoy amateur photography and sketching..
